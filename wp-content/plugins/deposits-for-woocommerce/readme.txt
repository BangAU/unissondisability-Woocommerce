== WooCommerce deposits Free Version Features ==
* Allowing customers to place Deposits
* Set-Up Fixed or Percentage Deposit Amount
* Allowing Customers to add Deposit and Regular products in one order
* Prevent customers from adding deposit products when they have regular items on their carts.
* Replace All Default Label & Messages from Setting panel.
* Support All WooCommerce Payment methods
* Suppotr for offline payments ( Direct bank transfer,Cash on delivery,Check payments )
* Customers can pay full or make a partial payment
* Custom Deposit Settings for each Products
* Support Variation Products
* Change radio box style
* Translation ready
* Sortable Deposit Report for admin
* New Deposit, Deposit Order & Deposit Paid email templates
